## Market Landscape

### Three Market Segments

#### 1. **AI Calorie/Macro Trackers**
Apps that use AI to identify foods from photos but focus on **calorie counting**, not plan compliance.

**Leaders:**
- MyFitnessPal
- Yazio
- Lose It!

**Pricing:** $0-$20/month

**Market Trend:** Users frustrated with increasing costs and premium-only features (MyFitnessPal losing users to Lose It!).

#### 2. **Dietitian Connection Platforms**
Platforms that connect users with nutritionists who create plans, but **lack automated compliance checking**.

**Leaders:**
- Nourish
- Fay
- Nutrium
- Healthie

**Pricing:** Varies (often insurance-covered or $50-150/month for coaching)

**Market Trend:** Clients want virtual care options and easy access to nutrition plans.

#### 3. **Photo-Based Coaching Apps**
Apps using meal photos with human coach review - **most similar to PlateCheck's value proposition**.

**Leaders:**
- Rise (acquired by One Medical, 2016)
- Ate App

**Pricing:** Bundled with services or subscription

**Market Trend:** Human-in-the-loop approach is expensive and slow.
